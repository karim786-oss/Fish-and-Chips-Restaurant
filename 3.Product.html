Fish and chips is a classic British dish consisting of battered and deep-fried fish, typically cod or haddock, served with thick-cut fried potatoes known as chips. Traditionally, it is seasoned with salt and vinegar and often accompanied by sides such as mushy peas, tartar sauce, and sometimes curry sauce. We offer different types of fish like, cod, haddock, plaice, rock & husk. It is special dish of our restaurant.
